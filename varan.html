Varanasi

The city is very commonly reffered to as City of Temples, Holy City of India. Religious Capital of India. and City of Learning.

Varanasi is one of the oldest living cities in the world. its Prominence in Hindu mythology is Virtually unrevealed. Mark Twain, The English Author and liturature, who was enthralled by the legendand santcity of Benaras, once wrote , "Benaras is older than history,older than tradition, older even than legend and looks twice as old as all of them put together."

The best time period for visiting Varanasi is from October to March, when the weather is not so hot and it is dry. November to February is the peak season, when crowds and higher prices are expected.Rainy season is from late June to September. It is still possible to tour Varanasi in these months, but get ready for the humidity and rain. Lower prices and less-crowded attractions are the rewards.

Varanasi is one of the seven sacred cities of Hinduism and is located in the state of Uttar Pradesh along the western banks of the Ganges River. Regarded as the spiritual capital of India, this city draws many religious pilgrims who come to bathe in the Ganges River's sacred waters and perform funeral rites.
